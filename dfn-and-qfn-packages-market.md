<h2>DFN and QFN Packages Market Overview</h2>

QFN (Quad Flat No-leads) and DFN (Dual Flat No-leads) are both emerging packaging technologies widely used in the semiconductor industry. These packages offer several advantages, including small size, low profile, and cost efficiency, making them ideal for applications in high-frequency, radio frequency, and various electronic systems.

QFN is a surface mount chip packaging technology that features:

•	Small pad size

•	Small volume

•	Plastic sealing material

•	Square or rectangular shape

The QFN package is particularly suitable for high-frequency and radio frequency applications due to its small footprint and efficient electrical performance.

<h2>DFN and QFN Packages Key Market Trends  :</h2>

•	Miniaturization of Electronic Devices

<h2>Isolation IC Market Overview</h2>

Isolation ICs (Isolation Integrated Circuits) are specialized semiconductor components that provide electrical isolation between the input and output of a circuit. These chips are essential in high-voltage systems, multi-circuit configurations, and applications where signal integrity, noise immunity, or safety regulations require effective electrical separation. Key functions include:

•	Insulation between high and low voltage circuits

•	Noise and interference isolation

•	Voltage level shifting and signal matching

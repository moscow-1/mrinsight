<h2>77GHz Radar Chip Market Overview</h2>

A 77GHz radar chip is a semiconductor device operating at 77 gigahertz, primarily used in automotive radar systems. These chips are critical for:

Adaptive cruise control

Collision avoidance

Blind-spot detection

Autonomous driving features

They transmit and receive electromagnetic signals to detect objects, measure distances, and provide essential data for vehicle safety and driver assistance systems.

<h2>Motorized Zoom Lens Market Overview</h2>

The global Motorized Zoom Lens market was valued at USD 321 million in 2024 and is projected to reach USD 589 million by 2031, growing at a CAGR of 9.3% during the forecast period (2024–2031).

A Motorized Zoom Lens is a type of camera lens that:

•	Allows for smooth focal length changes within a specific range through an electric drive mechanism

•	Enables users to adjust the shooting range without changing the shooting distance, via a simple button or knob operation

<h2>Motorized Zoom Lens Key Market Trends  :</h2>

•	Rising Adoption in City Surveillance:

Increasing urbanization and the need for advanced security systems are boosting demand for motorized zoom lenses in city surveillance applications.

•	Advancement in AI-based Autofocus Systems:

Integration of artificial intelligence is enabling smarter autofocus and subject-tracking functionalities in motorized zoom lenses.

•	Growth in Drone and Mobile Usage:

The growing use of drones and smartphones is driving the demand for compact and lightweight motorized zoom lenses with high performance.

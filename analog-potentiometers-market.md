<h2>Analog Potentiometers Market Overview</h2>

The analog potentiometer (also named mechanical potentiometer) basically consists of a resistor and wiper (brush). The resistor and wiper accurately convert the relative amount of displacement to a voltage output. It can be used in industrial control, medical equipment, automobiles, household appliances, consumer electronics and other applications.

<h2>Encoder Code Wheels Market Overview</h2>

An Encoder Code Wheel, or code wheel, is a precision mechanical component used in rotary and linear encoders to convert physical motion into digital signals. These wheels contain patterns of alternating opaque and transparent segments. As motion occurs, sensors detect light variations through the pattern, converting these into electrical signals that provide real-time feedback on speed, direction, and position. This makes them essential for motion tracking and control in various industrial and technological applications.

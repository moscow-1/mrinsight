<h2>Mask Defect Inspection Equipment Market Overview</h2>

Mask Defect Inspection Equipment is a pattern master used in photolithography in microelectronics manufacturing. An opaque light-shielding film is used to form a mask pattern on a transparent substrate, and the pattern is transferred to the product substrate through exposure.

<h2>RAID Controller Card Market Overview</h2>

A RAID controller card is a plug-in expansion card that connects to PCIe or PCI-X motherboard slots. It features:

•	A dedicated RAID processor

•	Integrated I/O processors

•	Multiple drive interfaces

These cards manage data redundancy and performance in storage systems, especially for enterprise environments.

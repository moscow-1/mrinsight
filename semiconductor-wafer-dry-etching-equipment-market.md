<h2>Semiconductor Wafer Dry Etching Equipment Market Overview</h2>

Semiconductor wafer dry etching equipment is a type of manufacturing tool used in the semiconductor industry for the process of etching or removing material from the surface of semiconductor wafers. Etching is a critical step in semiconductor device fabrication, allowing precise patterning of features on the wafer surface. Dry etching, as opposed to wet etching, involves using plasma or reactive gases to chemically or physically remove material from the wafer surface.

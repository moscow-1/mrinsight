<h2>Colored Glass Filters Market Overview</h2>

Colored glass filters are devices that selectively transmit light of different wavelengths. Typically implemented as a glass plane or plastic device in the optical path, these filters are either dyed in the bulk or have interference coatings. The optical properties of these filters are described by their frequency response, which specifies how the magnitude and phase of each frequency component of an incoming signal are modified by the filter.

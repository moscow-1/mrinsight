<h2>Passive Electronic Components Market Overview</h2>

Passive electronic components are essential building blocks in all electronic circuits. They do not require external power to operate and do not amplify signals.

<h2>Passive Electronic Components Key Market Trends  :</h2>

1.	Rising Demand for Consumer Electronics

The growing use of smartphones, laptops, smart TVs, and wearables is driving the demand for passive components, especially capacitors and resistors.

2.	Surge in Automotive Electronics

Electric vehicles (EVs), advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), and infotainment units are increasing the adoption of passive electronic components in the automotive sector.

3.	Miniaturization of Components

The trend toward compact and lightweight electronic devices is leading to increased demand for smaller, high-performance passive components.

<h2>IC Test Burn-in Board (BIB) Market Overview</h2>

IC Test Burn-in Board (BIB) is a specialized testing device used in the semiconductor industry to assess the reliability and performance of integrated circuits (ICs) before they are shipped for commercial use. The primary function of a burn-in board is to simulate the conditions under which the ICs will operate, subjecting them to stress testing to identify any potential failures, defects, or performance issues that may arise over time. This process helps ensure that only fully functional and reliable ICs are distributed to customers.

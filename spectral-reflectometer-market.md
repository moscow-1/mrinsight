<h2>Spectral Reflectometer Market Overview</h2>

A Spectral Reflectometer is an optical measurement device designed to analyze the reflective properties of surfaces or thin films across a broad range of wavelengths. By directing light onto a material and measuring the intensity of reflected light as a function of wavelength, it determines parameters such as film thickness, refractive index, and surface uniformity. Widely used in industries like semiconductors, optics, and coatings, spectral reflectometers are valued for their precision, non-destructive nature, and ability to provide real-time feedback in quality control and process monitoring.

<h2>Interactive Whiteboard Market Overview</h2>

The global Interactive Whiteboard Market was valued at USD 989 million in 2023 and is projected to reach USD 1,553 million by 2030, growing at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period.

An interactive whiteboard (IWB) is an instructional tool that allows computer images to be displayed onto a board using a digital projector. The instructor can manipulate elements on the board using their finger as a mouse, directly on the screen. Items can be dragged, clicked, copied, and handwritten notes can be transformed into text and saved. These boards are powerful tools in classrooms, enhancing interactivity and collaboration, enabling the integration of media content into lessons, and supporting collaborative learning. When used innovatively, IWBs create a wide range of learning opportunities.

<h2>Automated Test Equipment (ATE) Boards Market Overview</h2>

Automated Test Equipment (ATE) boards are crucial components used in testing the electrical performance of various devices, particularly in the semiconductor and electronics industries. These boards are designed to interface with devices under test (DUT) and provide the necessary electrical signals, measurements, and control to conduct automated testing. ATE systems are widely used for testing integrated circuits (ICs), semiconductor components, and other electronic devices during manufacturing and development stages. ATE boards provide the interface for connecting and communicating with the DUTs, executing test sequences, and analyzing test results.

<h2>Wafer Vacuum End Effector Market Overview</h2>

A Wafer Vacuum End Effector is a specialized robotic handling tool used in semiconductor manufacturing to safely pick up, transport, and place silicon wafers. It operates using vacuum suction to grip the wafer without causing contamination or damage, ensuring precise and efficient wafer handling in automated production processes.

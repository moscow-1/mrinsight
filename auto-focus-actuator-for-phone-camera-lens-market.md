<h2>Auto Focus Actuator for Phone Camera Lens Market Overview</h2>



Auto Focus Actuator are a very mature design that utilizes a magnet and yoke in conjunction with a coil. The coil and magnet are concentric about a common axis and there exists an engineered magnetic return path.

Auto Focus Actuator are applications of the principle of coils, which generate force when placed in a magnetic field and subjected to an electric current. Unlike ordinary motors, voice coil motors are based on rectilinear motion. Because the coil is the only moving component, they allow for both high-speed motion and accurate positioning.

<h2>300 mm Wafer Use Atomic Layer Deposition Equipment Key Market Trends  :</h2>

•	Shift Towards Advanced Node Technologies

Semiconductor manufacturers are increasingly using 300 mm wafers in advanced node technologies to meet the growing demand for smaller, faster, and more efficient chips.

•	Rising Adoption in Foundries

Foundries are major consumers of ALD equipment due to the surge in outsourcing of chip manufacturing, especially in Asia-Pacific regions.

•	Integration with AI and HPC

The rise of Artificial Intelligence and High-Performance Computing (HPC) is driving demand for ultra-thin, high-precision atomic layer deposition solutions.

•	Growth in PE-ALD Segment

Plasma-enhanced ALD (PE-ALD) is gaining momentum due to its capability to deposit high-quality films at lower temperatures.

•	Strong Presence in Asia-Pacific

Countries like China, Taiwan, and South Korea are dominating the global market, backed by strong investments and government support in semiconductor manufacturing.

<h2>Hall Effect Current Sensors Market Overview</h2>

Hall Effect Current Sensors are devices that measure electric current using the Hall effect principle. They work based on the proportional relationship between Hall voltage and magnetic field strength. When a constant control current is applied, the Hall voltage varies according to the magnetic field generated by the electric current, enabling accurate current measurement.

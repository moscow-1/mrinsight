<h2>Reflective Polarizer Film Market Overview</h2>

Reflective polarizer films are optical films used in LCDs to enhance brightness. These films:

•	Recycle light from the backlight system that would otherwise be absorbed

•	Increase screen brightness by approximately 50–60%

•	Selectively transmit light of one polarization state and reflect the other back into the backlight for reuse

<h2>Semiconductor Wafer Defect Inspection System Key Market Trends  :</h2>

Shift to 12-inch Wafers: As the semiconductor industry moves towards larger wafer sizes, the demand for high-speed and high-accuracy defect inspection systems is growing.

Increased Automation in Wafer Inspection: Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) is becoming more common, improving throughput and reducing labor costs in wafer defect detection.

Advances in E-beam and X-ray Inspection: The adoption of electron beam and X-ray inspection systems is on the rise for more detailed and accurate defect identification, especially for non-patterned wafers.

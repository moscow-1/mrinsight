<h2>Intelligent Power Module (IPM) Interfaces Optocouplers Market Overview:</h2>

Intelligent Power Module (IPM) Interfaces Optocouplers are semiconductor components used to provide isolation and safe signal transmission between high-voltage and low-voltage circuits. These devices are critical in systems involving high-power switching, offering short propagation delay, fast IGBT switching capabilities, high common mode transient immunity (CMTI), and operation over wide temperature ranges. Reinforced insulation makes them well-suited for interfacing with IPMs in motor drives, power inverters, and other control systems.

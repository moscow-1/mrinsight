<h2>2D AOI Inspection Systems Key Market Trends  :</h2>

•	Increased Adoption in PCB Manufacturing

The demand for 2D AOI inspection systems is rising rapidly in PCB production lines due to the growing need for precise and fast defect detection.

•	Shift Towards Inline AOI Systems

Inline 2D AOI systems are gaining popularity over offline models as manufacturers aim to enhance production efficiency and real-time defect monitoring.

•	Integration with Smart Factory Solutions

AOI systems are being integrated into smart manufacturing ecosystems, improving data collection and enabling predictive maintenance.

•	Miniaturization of Components

The trend towards miniaturization in electronics is pushing the demand for high-resolution inspection systems capable of detecting microscopic defects.

•	R&D Focus on Faster and Smarter Algorithms

Companies are investing in software development for AOI systems, focusing on AI-powered defect classification to reduce false positives and improve accuracy.

<h2>Artificial Tissue Chips Market Overview:</h2>

Artificial Tissue Chips are 3D microfluidic devices that mimic the physiological functions and mechanics of human organs or organ systems. These chips allow for the study of human biology in a highly controlled and accurate environment, which could one day replace animal testing in biomedical research. The technology’s ability to simulate multiple organ systems in a small-scale, high-throughput format makes it an essential tool for drug discovery, toxicology studies, and disease modeling.

4.	Applications in Personalized Medicine: Artificial Tissue Chips are playing a significant role in the growing field of personalized medicine, where drugs can be tested on tissue chips made from a patient’s own cells, leading to more targeted treatments.

<h2>Semiconductor Wafer Handling System Market Overview</h2>
  
Semiconductor Wafer Handling Systems are critical components in the semiconductor manufacturing process, facilitating the efficient movement and handling of semiconductor wafers throughout various stages of production. These systems are primarily divided into two main types: Atmospheric Transport Systems and Vacuum Transport Systems. These technologies are integral to maintaining the cleanliness, efficiency, and integrity of the manufacturing process.

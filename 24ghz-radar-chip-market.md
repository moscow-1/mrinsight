<h2>24GHz Radar Chip Market Overview</h2>

A 24GHz radar chip is a semiconductor device designed to operate at a frequency of 24 gigahertz. It is widely used in:

Automotive radar systems

Industrial sensors

Traffic monitoring

Short-range radar applications

These chips transmit and receive electromagnetic signals at 24GHz to detect objects, measure distances, and provide critical sensing data for a variety of applications.

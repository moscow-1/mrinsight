<h2>60GHz Radar Chip Market Overview</h2>

A 60GHz radar chip is a semiconductor device designed to operate at a frequency of 60 gigahertz. These radar chips are commonly used in applications such as automotive radar systems, industrial sensors, wireless communications, and short-range radar applications. They transmit and receive electromagnetic signals at 60GHz to detect objects, measure distances, and provide critical data for sensing, communication, and detection purposes.

<h2>Interactive Smartboards Market Overview</h2>

Interactive Smartboards are advanced interactive display products that integrate multiple functions such as LCD display, screen touch, audio and video playback, computer input, storage, and output. Using touch technology, these smartboards allow users to control the displayed content, enabling real-time human-computer interaction. They are ideal for one-to-many interactive display scenarios, enhancing engagement and collaboration. Visually, they resemble large flat-panel TVs and are considered more advanced than traditional interactive whiteboards, functioning as all-in-one devices.

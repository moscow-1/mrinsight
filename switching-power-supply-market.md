<h2>Switching Power Supply Market Overview</h2>

A Switched-Mode Power Supply (SMPS)—also known as a switching-mode power supply or switcher—is an electronic power supply that utilizes a switching regulator to convert electrical power with high efficiency.

Key Features:

•	Converts AC or DC input to regulated DC output

•	Efficiently changes voltage and current levels

•	Commonly used in applications such as personal computers, servers, and consumer electronics

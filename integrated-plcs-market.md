<h2>Integrated PLCs Key Market Trends  :</h2>

•	Rising Demand for Smart Automation

The growing need for efficient industrial automation is driving the adoption of integrated PLCs with built-in communication and IoT capabilities.

•	Expansion in Industrial IoT (IIoT)

Integrated PLCs are increasingly being used in IIoT applications, enabling real-time monitoring, predictive maintenance, and seamless connectivity.

•	Growing Adoption in Automotive & Power Sectors

Industries like automotive and power are significantly investing in integrated PLCs to enhance productivity and operational efficiency.

•	Increased Focus on Modular PLCs

The modular PLC segment is experiencing higher demand due to its scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness in industrial applications.

•	Advancements in Human-Machine Interfaces (HMI)

Integration of HMI with PLCs is improving ease of use, allowing operators to manage complex processes efficiently.

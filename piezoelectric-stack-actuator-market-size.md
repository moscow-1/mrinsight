Fast response speed

High control accuracy

High output torque density

Low operating noise

Growing Adoption in Automotive Industry: The increasing use of piezoelectric stack actuators in automotive applications, especially in new energy vehicles, is driving market growth. Technological Advancements in Precision Positioning: Continuous developments in piezoelectric stack actuator technology are improving performance in precision positioning systems, further expanding their applications in industries like manufacturing and optical instruments.

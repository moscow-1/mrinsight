<h2>Equipment Front End Module (EFEM) Key Market Trends  :</h2>

•	Increased Demand for 300mm Wafers: The demand for 300mm wafers continues to drive the growth of the EFEM market, with 95% of the market share. This trend is expected to continue as semiconductor manufacturing processes demand larger wafer sizes.

•	Technological Advancements in Automation: Automation technology is being increasingly integrated into EFEM systems to enhance efficiency and reduce manual labor, making them more attractive to manufacturers.

•	Rise of 3-Port EFEM: The 3-port EFEM category dominates the market with 50% of the share, indicating the rising preference for more versatile and efficient solutions in semiconductor wafer handling.

•	Expansion in Asian Markets: With Japan, China, and Southeast Asia being major production hubs, the market is seeing significant growth in the Asia-Pacific region due to the increasing semiconductor manufacturing activities.

•	Shift Towards Smaller and Cost-Effective Modules: Manufacturers are focusing on producing smaller and more cost-effective EFEM models to cater to mid-range applications and reduce operational costs.

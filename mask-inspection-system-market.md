<h2>Mask Inspection System Market Overview</h2>

Mask inspection systems are vital tools in semiconductor manufacturing, used to detect defects and irregularities on photomasks during the production process. These systems ensure that masks, which are used in photolithography, are free from defects that could impact the quality and performance of semiconductor devices.

<h2>Foldable Microphone Array Market Overview</h2>

A foldable microphone array is a specially designed acoustic measurement device that combines the features of a microphone array with a foldable structure. A foldable microphone array consists of multiple microphones arranged in a certain pattern, which can simultaneously capture sound signals from multiple different points and perform spatial audio filtering to generate the direction of the sound waves. Its key feature is its foldability, which allows the array to be easily folded up when not in use to reduce volume and facilitate transportation. Due to its portability and flexibility, the foldable microphone array is very suitable for measuring large outdoor sound sources, such as noise monitoring at concerts, sporting events, etc.

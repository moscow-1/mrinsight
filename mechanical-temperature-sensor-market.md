<h2>Mechanical Temperature Sensor Market Overview</h2>



Mechanical Temperature Sensor, also known as a mechanical thermostat, is a device used to measure and regulate temperature based on physical changes in its components in response to temperature fluctuations. Unlike electronic temperature sensors, which rely on electronic components to measure temperature, mechanical temperature sensors use mechanical mechanisms to achieve temperature sensing and control.

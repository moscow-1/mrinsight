Silicon Materials for Etching Equipment refer to high-purity silicon components used in semiconductor manufacturing processes, specifically in etching equipment. These materials include silicon electrodes, silicon rings, and other silicon-based parts that help in the precise removal of material layers from semiconductor wafers. They play a crucial role in achieving high accuracy, uniformity, and durability in advanced wafer fabrication.

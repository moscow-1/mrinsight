<h2>High Purity Germanium Detector Market Overview</h2>

A High Purity Germanium (HPGe) Detector is a type of semiconductor radiation detector that utilizes highly purified germanium (Ge) crystals to detect ionizing radiation such as gamma rays and X-rays. These detectors are essential in applications that demand precise measurement and radiation analysis, including nuclear physics, medical imaging, environmental monitoring, and security.

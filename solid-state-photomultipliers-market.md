<h2>Solid-State Photomultipliers Market Overview:</h2>

Solid-State Photomultipliers (SSPMs) are advanced photon-sensitive devices capable of detecting signals from ultraviolet to infrared with high gain and sensitivity. These devices use arrays of avalanche photodiodes connected together to enable precise single-photon counting, high-resolution detection of low-light and radiation signals, and enhanced operational efficiency. With features like low bias voltage, insensitivity to magnetic fields, and compact structure, SSPMs are becoming critical components in medical, automotive, research, and security applications.

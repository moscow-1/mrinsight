<h2>Thermo Electric Modules and Assemblies Market Overview:</h2>

Thermo Electric Modules and Assemblies are semiconductor devices that use thermoelectric effects to convert heat energy into electricity or to create temperature differences for cooling purposes. These devices work by applying a low DC voltage at both ends of the thermoelectric cooler, causing heat to flow from one side to the other. As the demand for energy-efficient and sustainable solutions increases across industries such as automotive, aerospace, medical devices, and consumer electronics, thermoelectric technology has become increasingly essential for temperature control, energy harvesting, and other applications.

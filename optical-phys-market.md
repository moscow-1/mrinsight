<h2>Optical PHYs Market Overview</h2>

Optical PHYs (Physical Layer Devices) are essential components in optical communication systems, facilitating the transmission and reception of data over optical fiber networks. These devices enable high-speed, long-distance, and low-latency data communication, making them crucial for applications in data centers, telecommunications, 5G infrastructure, industrial automation, and automotive connectivity.

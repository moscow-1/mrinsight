<h2>Semiconductor Etching Agents Market Overview</h2>

Semiconductor etchants are specialized electronic chemicals used to selectively remove material during the etching process in semiconductor manufacturing. Etching is a critical step in producing microelectronic components like integrated circuits (ICs), solar cells, and display panels.

The type of etchant varies based on the substrate material and etching method (wet or dry).

Etchants are formulated to control etch rate, selectivity, and anisotropy, ensuring precision in microfabrication.

<h2>IC Foundry Key Market Trends  :</h2>

Increased Demand for Advanced Nodes

There is a growing demand for advanced process nodes like 5nm and 3nm, driven by the rise in applications such as AI, IoT, and 5G.

Rise of Foundry Consolidation

Major players like TSMC and Samsung Foundry are dominating the market, leading to consolidation and increased market share for a few key companies.

Shift Toward 12-inch Wafer Production

The transition to 12-inch wafers is gaining momentum due to their higher yield and cost efficiency compared to 8-inch wafers.

Growing Importance of Specialty Technologies

Specialty technologies, such as MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) and RF (Radio Frequency) chips, are becoming increasingly important for sectors like automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics.

<h2>GaN-on-Si Wafer Market Overview</h2>

Gallium nitride (GaN) is used as the wide band gap material for next generation power semiconductors and high frequency devices. Gallium Nitride on Silicon wafers are used for both Power and 5F/RF application, D-mode GaN-on-Si HEMT & E-mode GaN-on-Si HEMT, etc. Currently the key size is 6 inch GaN Si wafer, and the 8 inch GaN Si wafer will grow faster.

Gallium Nitride on Silicon (GaN-on-Si) wafers are increasingly used in high-performance electronics, offering advantages such as higher efficiency, faster switching speeds, and better thermal performance. They are widely adopted in applications ranging from consumer electronics to power devices and RF communications.

<h2>Magnetic Angular Position Sensors Market Overview</h2>

Magnetic Angular Position Sensors detect the angular position of rotating components using magnetic field-based measurement techniques. These sensors provide precise, non-contact, and reliable positioning information, making them essential in automotive systems, industrial machinery, medical devices, and aerospace applications.

<h2>RF Power Supply for Semiconductor Market Overview</h2>

An RF Power Supply for Semiconductor is designed to generate a sine wave voltage at a fixed frequency within the radio frequency (RF) range, typically between 3 kHz and 300 GHz.

Power Range: Generally between 0.3 kW and 15 kW

Frequency Range: Includes frequencies such as 2 MHz, 4 kHz, 13.56 MHz, 27.12 MHz, 40.68 MHz, 60 MHz, and more.

These supplies are used to produce stable current and voltage, ensuring reliable operation of various semiconductor manufacturing equipment.

Increased Demand for High-Frequency Power SuppliesAs the semiconductor industry advances, there is a growing need for high-frequency RF power supplies. Frequencies like 13.56 MHz and 27.12 MHz are expected to see strong demand due to their application in etching and CVD processes.

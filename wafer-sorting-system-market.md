<h2>Wafer Sorting System Market Overview</h2>

Wafer sort is a crucial process in semiconductor wafer processing facilities. It is the first testing stop for completed wafers. The test results provide valuable feedback to multiple departments  production control, wafer fabrication, product engineering and quality control. If yield results are low then changes have to be made. If yields are high or as expected the process is in control and no changes are required.

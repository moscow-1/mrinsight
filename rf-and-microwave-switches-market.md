<h2>RF and Microwave Switches Market Overview</h2>

RF (Radio Frequency) and Microwave Switches are critical components used to route high-frequency signals through various transmission paths. These switches are widely used in microwave test systems for efficient signal routing between instruments and Devices Under Test (DUTs).

Key Functions:

•	Signal Routing: Directs signals from multiple instruments to one or more DUTs.

•	Test Automation: Allows the automation of testing procedures in high-volume production environments.

•	Increased Throughput: Reduces manual intervention, minimizing connects/disconnects and enhancing testing efficiency.

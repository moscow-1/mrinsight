<h2>Vacuum Cable Market Overview</h2>

Vacuum cables are specially designed electrical cables that maintain high-performance insulation and stable signal transmission under extreme vacuum conditions such as HV (High Vacuum), UHV (Ultra-High Vacuum), and XHV (Extreme High Vacuum).

Typically, polyimide is used as the insulation material due to its excellent chemical stability, electrical insulation, and mechanical durability.

These cables are essential in industries where reliability under vacuum conditions is critical.

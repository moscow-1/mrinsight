<h2>Polyimide Electrostatic Chuck Market Overview</h2>

Polyimide Type Electrostatic Chucks (ESCs) are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, designed to securely hold wafers or substrates during various production processes. Unlike ceramic ESCs, which are made from materials like alumina or aluminum nitride, polyimide ESCs use polyimide, a high-performance polymer, as the dielectric material.

<h2>Miniature Camera Modules Key Market Trends  :</h2>

•	Growing Integration in Smartphones and Wearables

The need for high-resolution and ultra-compact camera modules is increasing as smartphones and wearables demand slimmer and multifunctional designs.

•	Rise in Automotive Applications

Miniature cameras are being increasingly adopted in ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems), reversing cameras, and in-cabin monitoring systems.

•	Surging Demand in Healthcare Devices

Medical imaging and diagnostic devices are integrating miniature camera modules for precision-based internal examinations.

•	Shift Towards CMOS Imaging Over CCD

Due to advantages like lower power consumption and faster image processing, CMOS sensors are gradually replacing traditional CCDs.

•	Technological Advancements in Low-Light Imaging

Manufacturers are focusing on innovations such as better night vision and low-light performance for security, automotive, and surveillance use.

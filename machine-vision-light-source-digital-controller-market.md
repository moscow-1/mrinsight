<h2>Machine Vision Light Source Digital Controller Market Overview</h2>

The machine vision light source digital controller is a device that uses digital signals to precisely control the light source in a machine vision system. It receives digital instructions from the machine vision system or other control devices to manage brightness, lighting status (on/off), and stroboscopic characteristics with high precision.

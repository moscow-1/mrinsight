<h2>Rigid-Flex PCBs (RFPC) Market Overview</h2>

Rigid-flex PCBs (Printed Circuit Boards) are a hybrid structure that combines rigid and flexible circuit board technologies into a single interconnect structure. They are widely used in various high-performance electronic products due to their unique advantages, including flexibility, reliability, and space-saving capabilities. Typically, the flexible portion is made of polyimide, while the rigid section is constructed from materials like FR4 or polyimide laminate.

The rigid-flex PCB market has been experiencing robust growth, driven by technological innovations, expanding application areas, and increased consumer demand across various industries.

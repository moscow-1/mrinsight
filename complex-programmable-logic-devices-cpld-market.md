<h2>Complex Programmable Logic Devices (CPLD) Market Overview</h2>

CPLDs are programmable logic devices that bridge the gap between PALs (Programmable Array Logic) and FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays).

They incorporate architectural features of both PALs and FPGAs.

The core building block of a CPLD is the macrocell, which executes logic based on disjunctive normal form expressions and supports specialized operations.

<h2>Complex Programmable Logic Devices (CPLD) Key Market Trends  :</h2>

Rise in Demand for Low Power, High Performance Devices

CPLDs are increasingly preferred for applications needing ultra-low power and instant-on capabilities, especially in portable and industrial automation equipment.

Integration with IoT and Smart Devices

With the expansion of IoT and Industry 4.0, CPLDs are becoming essential in smart sensors and embedded control systems.

Surge in Embedded System Applications

The growth of embedded systems across automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial sectors is driving CPLD adoption.

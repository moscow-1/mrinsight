<h2>Indoor Web Cameras Key Market Trends</h2>

•	Increased Demand for Surveillance Cameras – The rise in security concerns has led to a higher adoption of outdoor web cameras in residential and commercial sectors.

•	Integration of AI and IoT – AI-powered analytics and IoT connectivity are improving the functionality of outdoor web cameras, making them smarter and more efficient.

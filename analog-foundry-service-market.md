<h2>Analog Foundry Service Market Overview</h2>

Analog integrated circuits (ICs) play a crucial role in modern electronics by processing real-world signals such as:

•	Sound

•	Temperature

•	Pressure

•	Light/images

These signals are conditioned, amplified, and often converted into digital data to be processed by digital semiconductors like microcontrollers and embedded processors. Additionally, analog ICs manage power conversion, storage, distribution, isolation, and measurement, making them essential across nearly all electronic devices, whether powered by batteries or electricity.

As digitization accelerates, analog chips remain indispensable, acting as critical interfaces between humans, the physical world, and digital devices.

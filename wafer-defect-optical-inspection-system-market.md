<h2>Wafer Defect Optical Inspection System Market Overview</h2>

A wafer defect inspection system plays a crucial role in semiconductor manufacturing by detecting physical and pattern defects on wafers. These systems help identify defects like foreign particles and flaws in circuit patterns, crucial for ensuring the quality and functionality of semiconductor devices. The system locates defects and provides precise position coordinates (X, Y) to guide corrective actions.

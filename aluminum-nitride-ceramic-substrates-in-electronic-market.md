Material Insights

Aluminum Nitride (AlN) is a covalently-bonded ceramic material known for:

•	Very high thermal conductivity

•	Excellent electrical insulation

•	Synthesized from aluminum and nitrogen, both of which are abundant in nature

These properties make AlN ceramic substrates ideal for high-performance electronic applications.

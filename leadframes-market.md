<h2>Leadframes Market Overview</h2>

Lead frames are essential components in semiconductor packages, responsible for delivering electrical signals to external circuits and providing mechanical support to the chip.

Structure of a Lead Frame:

•	Die Mounting Paddle:

Mechanically supports the semiconductor die during the packaging process.

•	Lead Fingers:

Serve as the electrical connection between the die and external circuitry.
